# Week 1: Our Plan

This week we ideate. The goal: by **Thursday** we have one shared plan, and **Monday** we hit the ground running.

Nobody ships code yet. This week is for thinking, investigating, and deciding **together**. The pieces connect, so you cannot finalize yours in a vacuum:

```
Wendell finds/collects the data  ->  Kanayo models it  ->  De'Andre visualizes it
                                                                  |
                              Chase builds the site  <-  James designs it
```

De'Andre's visualizations decide what data we need. That shapes Kanayo's data model. That shapes what Wendell collects. Talk to each other.

## Your first task is also your first git lesson

Each of you owns a planning doc below. This week you will:

1. Create a branch named `your-role/ideation` (for example `data-viz/ideation`).
2. Edit your doc with your thinking.
3. Open a Pull Request into `main` and request Dr. Scruse.
4. She merges it, and your change goes **live on this very page**. Refresh and see it.

That is the exact workflow we use all summer. You are learning it now on your own planning doc, where nothing can break.

## What we are building (the shared picture)

A public website telling the story of the REACH consortium two ways:

- **By institution:** the 12 HBCU partners, who they are, what they contributed.
- **By workstream:** the five workstreams (WS1 Foundational AI, WS2 Applied AI, WS3 AI Safety, WS4 AI Policy, WS5 Dissemination), what each produced, and which institutions drove them.

The benchmark findings come later and plug into the same site. We do not wait on them.
